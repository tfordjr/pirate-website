BlackBeard

1680 - Nov 22, 1718

Edward Teach (alternatively spelled Edward Thatch, better known as Blackbeard, was an English pirate who operated around the West Indies and the eastern coast of Britain's North American colonies.

Calico Jack

December 26, 1682 – November 18, 1720

John Rackham, commonly known as Calico Jack, was an English pirate captain operating in the Bahamas and in Cuba during the early 18th century. His nickname was derived from the calico clothing that he wore.

Anne Bonny

March 8, 1697 – disappeared April 1721

Anne Bonny, sometimes Anne Bonney, was an Irish pirate operating in the Caribbean, and one of a few female pirates in recorded history.

Henry Avery

Aug 23, 1659 - 1699

Henry Every, also known as Henry Avery, sometimes erroneously given as Jack Avery or John Avery, was an English pirate who operated in the Atlantic and Indian oceans in the mid-1690s.
